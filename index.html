WAAYnft

 

The Collection

WAAY NFT is a collection of 2,222 unique NFTs living in the shadows of the Ethereum blockchain. Through an algorithmic process, each NFT is created with a distinct combination of over a hundred hand-drawn unique traits, including hats, eyes, hands, and much more. The result is a completely one-of-a-kind and personalized NFT. Hidden in the shadows for so long, it's time they reveal themselves to the world!

Pedro Friedberg

Pedro Friedeberg is a contemporary Mexican artist best known for his Surrealist sculptures and prints. His eclectic body of work bridges architectural imagery and psychedelic patterns with occult iconography. Best known for his Hand Chair (1961), the artist melds practical furniture design into the shape of a giant hand.

 
 

Our Mission

At WAAY NFT, we're committed to democratizing digital art in Mexico and empowering emerging artists. Our vision breaks economic, social and geographical barriers, providing an inclusive platform where passionate collectors and promising artists converge. Through high-quality NFTs and access to exclusive physical artwork we connect art enthusiasts with extraordinary expressions, redefining the way we experience, share and value art.
